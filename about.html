About

Multilingual speaking communities have always been present in the world,
due to colonisation and migrations, or as dialects coexisting with other languages.
While in our everyday lives it could be inconvenient to mix different languages,
poetry is a realm where instant communication is not necessary, and when it turns into music, different languages can work together to create new sounds, new meanings.

Multilingual lyrics are also a way for artists to express their identity and their influences,
they are a testament to the multiculturality of our society.

This website is a collection of multilingual songs of different origins, which can be added to, modified, creating visual weavings and longer texts that interlace with each other.
